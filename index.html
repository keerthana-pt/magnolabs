Firebase Notification Scheduler, FNS helps you to schedule and manage Firebase Notification regardless of your tech platform.

Why FNS?

At magnolabs (the parent company of FNS), we were trying to schedule firebase cloud messages/Firebase push notifications. There was no solution to handle this back then in 2016. We handled this on the device knowing its drawbacks hoping firebase or someone will else will come up with a solution. But unfortunately, this problem is left unsolved and we’ve decided to solve it for ourselves and all developers.

How Does It Works?

  1. Register and get access key
    Signup to our Rapid API and get access
    key’s for your app.

  2. Schedule notifications via API
    Schedule your notifications via our REST API
    from your mobile app or backend service.

  3. Deliver notifications on time
    We will deliver the push notifications at the
    scheduled times to your apps.
